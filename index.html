Privacy Policy – HexDash

Last updated: June 26, 2026

HexDash ("the Game") is a free arcade game developed by Efe. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

The Game does not require account registration and does not collect personally identifiable information directly.

  • Local game data: Scores, settings, purchased items, and skin preferences are stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers.
    • details.

2. Advertising

The Game displays banner and interstitial ads served by Google AdMob. If you are located in the EEA or UK, a consent dialog will appear before any personalized ads are shown. You may change your advertising preferences at any time via the Privacy Options link in the main menu.

3. Children

The Game is rated for users aged 3 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by Google AdMob's advertising services.

5. Data Retention

All locally stored data remains on your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the Game.
